Our Story

Meet Chef Marco Rossi

Born in the heart of Milan and trained in the legendary kitchens of Osteria Francescana, Chef Marco Rossi brings over 25 years of culinary mastery to La Bella Vita. His journey began at age 14 in his grandmother's trattoria, where he learned that great Italian cooking is not just about technique – it's about love, tradition, and respect for ingredients.

After earning his first Michelin star at age 28, Marco traveled throughout Italy, from the truffle-rich hills of Piedmont to the sun-drenched coasts of Sicily, collecting recipes and stories from nonnas and master chefs alike. Each dish at La Bella Vita reflects this rich tapestry of regional Italian cuisine.

"For me, cooking is poetry," Marco says. "Every ingredient has a voice, every dish tells a story. When you dine at La Bella Vita, you're not just eating – you're experiencing generations of Italian passion and tradition on your plate."

Awards: Michelin Star (2015-2019) • James Beard Award Nominee • Best Italian Chef 2023
